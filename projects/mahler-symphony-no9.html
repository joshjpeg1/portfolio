Mahler Symphony no.9

about

After researching Swiss design for several months, it was time to put the concepts to use. For my typography class, I was tasked with creating a poster for an event for the Boston Symphony Orchestra. After creating several designs, I chose this arrangement, as it creates visual interest by incorporating the cellist as an element in the design, placing text behind her to create an illusion of depth.
