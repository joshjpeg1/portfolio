The Ills of Technology

about

I use technology everyday, be it the phone in my pocket, the watch on my wrist, or the computers I produce my work on. Although, I always appreciate the advancements we have made and the positive impacts it's had on our lives, it's important not to let it consume us. This series of paintings explores that idea, reminding us not to forget the world and the people closest to us.
